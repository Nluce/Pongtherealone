Pong by Navarre Luce

This version of pong is a 2 player game.

The first player to reach 11 points is the winner!

The ball will be served to the player 
who won the last round.


Instructions
Player 1 uses the "W" and "S" keys to move the paddle, and player 2 uses
the up and down arrow keys.

Use the indicated keys to navigate the menus.
